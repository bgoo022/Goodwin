About Us

At Stonybrook Wiltshires, we take pride in breeding sheep that require minimal maintenance while thriving in various conditions. Our breeding strategy focuses on natural wool shedding, facial eczema resistance, and parasite tolerance, ensuring our sheep are both productive and cost-effective.
